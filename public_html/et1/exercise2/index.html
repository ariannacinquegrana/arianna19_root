Exercise 2: Fix/Handy Project

I 3D printed a coaster!

I decided to 3D print a coaster because it's functional and it's something that I would use a lot. I also thought it was a unique idea and I thought it would be cool to add it to my living room. Designing the coaster was simple and fast to get done. I designed a big circle and four little circles under the big circle for it to stand.
